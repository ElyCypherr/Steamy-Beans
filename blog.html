Welcome to Steamy Beans’ Blog – “Bringing the heat to your cup”

At Steamy Beans, we’re passionate about providing our customers with the highest quality coffee beans, brewed with precision and care. We’ve been in the coffee business for over a decade, and we’re proud of the journey we’ve taken to get where we are today.

We pride ourselves on using premium ingredients that are sourced from local farmers, ensuring that our products are of the highest quality. Our team of expert baristas and bakers use their skills to create unique and delicious drinks and pastries that cater to every taste bud.

The History of Steamy Beans:

May 13, 2004

Our story begins in 2004 when our founder, Kristiana, had a vision to create an online café that would allow people to enjoy great coffee from the comfort of their own homes. With this vision in mind, Kristiana launched Steamy Beans’ online store, offering a variety of coffee blends and brewing equipment.

At first, it was a slow start. But, as word spread about the quality of our coffee and the convenience of our online store, business began to pick up. Our loyal customers soon became our biggest advocates, spreading the word about Steamy Beans to their friends and family.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our online store to include a wider variety of coffee blends, brewing equipment, and accessories, but we’ve never lost sight of our commitment to quality and customer service. We’re proud to source our coffee beans from ethical and sustainable sources, ensuring that our coffee not only tastes great but also supports the farmers who grow it.

Today, Steamy Beans’ online café is more than just an online store – we’re a community of coffee lovers from all over the world. We’re constantly striving to improve and innovate, whether it’s through introducing new blends, offering brewing tips and tutorials, or hosting virtual coffee tastings.

We’re excited to continue on this journey and can’t wait to see what the future holds for Steamy Beans. Thank you for supporting us and being a part of our story.
